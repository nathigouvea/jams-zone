# jams.zone — Business Plan
*Created: 2026-03-17 | Owner: Nathielle Gouvea + Moshi #3*

---

## 🎯 Mission
jams.zone is a digital product studio selling premium templates, UI kits, developer tools, and AI-native resources for designers, developers, and freelancers worldwide.

## 🧠 Positioning
**"Premium digital products. Built fast. Priced fairly."**

- Not a marketplace (like Creative Market or Gumroad) — a brand
- Not a freelancer portfolio — a product business
- Target: designers, developers, and freelancers who want to work smarter

## 👥 Target Customers
1. **Freelance web designers** — need templates to deliver faster
2. **Solo developers** — want boilerplates and starter kits
3. **Agency owners** — need client-ready templates and proposals
4. **AI-curious designers** — want Cursor rules and AI workflow setups
5. **Notion power users** — want dashboards and productivity systems

## 💰 Revenue Streams

### Primary: Digital Product Sales
| Category | Products | Price Range | Platform |
|----------|---------|-------------|----------|
| HTML/CSS Templates | SaaS, Agency, Portfolio, Restaurant | €19–39 | Gumroad, jams.zone |
| Framer Templates | Same categories rebuilt in Framer | €29–49 | Framer Marketplace |
| Notion Templates | Freelancer OS, Client CRM, Project Tracker | €9–19 | Gumroad, Etsy |
| UI Kits (Figma) | Icon sets, component libraries | €19–49 | Gumroad, Creative Market |
| Dev Tools | Cursor rules, Next.js boilerplates | €9–19 | Gumroad, jams.zone |
| AI Resources | Prompt packs, workflow guides | €9–19 | Gumroad |
| Bundles | All templates / All tools | €49–99 | Gumroad, jams.zone |

### Secondary: Affiliate/Referral
- Framer Partner Program: 50% of referred subscriptions for 12 months
- Resend, Netlify, other tools referrals

### Tertiary (Month 3+): Freelance clients via jams.zone contact
- Clients find jams.zone, love the work, hire Nathielle directly
