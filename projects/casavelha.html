Our Philosophy

"Food is the language of love spoken in every culture."

Casa Velha began as a family secret — a table in our grandmother's kitchen where neighbours became lifelong friends. Today we carry that same warmth into every plate, sourcing from the same farms, fishing villages, and wine cellars that have trusted us for four decades.

The Space

A dining room
steeped in history

Set within a 19th-century Porto townhouse, our dining room breathes history from every corner. Exposed azulejo tiles, candlelit arches, and the soft murmur of conversation create an atmosphere unlike any other in the city.
