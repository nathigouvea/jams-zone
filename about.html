About jams.zone

Built by a designer who got tired of charging hourly.

jams.zone started as a side experiment: what if I spent one day building a template instead of one week building a client project? It worked better than expected.

The story

I'm Nathielle Gouvea, a front-end developer and web designer based in Porto, Portugal. I've been building websites for clients since 2019 — everything from small business landing pages to e-commerce stores and SaaS products.

In 2026, I started using AI tools seriously in my workflow. Not as a gimmick, but as a genuine productivity multiplier. Cursor AI, Claude, and a well-crafted .cursorrules file changed how fast I could ship quality work.

That's when jams.zone was born. If I could build a landing page in a day instead of a week, I could also build a template in a weekend. And instead of billing one client for that work, I could sell it to a hundred people who need the same thing.

What we make

Everything on jams.zone is built the way I'd build a client project: with proper semantic HTML, real responsive design, thoughtful copy, and attention to the details that matter. No lorem ipsum. No placeholder garbage. Things that actually work.

We're starting with HTML templates, Notion dashboards, and developer tools. If it helps designers and developers work faster and charge more, we'll build it.

The AI thing

Yes, we use AI to build faster. That's not a secret or a shortcut — it's the workflow. The AI handles boilerplate and repetition. The design thinking, the copy, the quality decisions — those are human. The result is products that would take 3× longer to build traditionally, sold at prices that reflect that efficiency.

If you want to learn the workflow, check our Cursor AI guide.
